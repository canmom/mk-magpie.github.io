We believe in an eternal and infinite space of ideas and memories, a boundless heaven, that runs throughout and beyond all human existence. We believe that when our bodies die, we will remain in this space, our memories carried forth by all those who knew and loved us, and whatever remains of us will be at peace. We venerate two Divinities drawn out from boundless heaven, each twofold in herself - Marya-Kubileya, the wild and infinitely loving Mother, and Attis-Yeshua, the human and wounded and reborn sibling to us all. In Marya-Kubileya's name we seek comfort, forgiveness for our mistakes, and the inspiration to forgive and care for others. In Attis-Yeshua's name we seek a way to endure the inevitable suffering of life and transform ourselves through cathartic pain, and a mirror with which we may recognise and love ourselves in the hearts of those around us.

We believe that all people are worthy of love and forgiveness if they wish to better themselves, and that radical self-transformation is a source of great beauty, power, and understanding. We put no stock in ideas of sin, need for atonement before a spiritual authority, or fated punishment beyond this life, as Marya-Kubileya is all-forgiving. We value humility, curiosity, selflessness, and respect for all. We recognise that our Divinities are not the only lens through which these same values may be seen and attained, but hold their worship high as a uniquely resonant path for those with eyes to see it. We remember that we are mortal and finite, and though we must never stop trying, we can never reach true perfection; the striving itself is our Mother's great gift to us. We see that there is nothing without our Goddess. *Nihil sine Dea.*
